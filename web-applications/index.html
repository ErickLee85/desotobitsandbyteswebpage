Frequently Asked Questions

What technologies do you use for web applications?

We use modern, battle-tested technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, and various databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Firebase). We select the best tech stack based on your specific needs, scalability requirements, and budget to ensure optimal performance and maintainability.

How long does it take to build a web application?

Timeline varies based on complexity. A simple dashboard or MVP can take 3-4 weeks, while a comprehensive platform with multiple features, integrations, and custom functionality typically takes 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed project timeline after understanding your requirements.

Can you integrate with existing systems and APIs?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating with third-party services, existing databases, payment processors, CRM systems, and custom APIs. Whether it's Stripe, Salesforce, or your proprietary system, we'll ensure seamless data flow and functionality.

What kind of web applications do you build?

We build a wide range of web applications including SaaS platforms, CRM systems, admin dashboards, e-commerce solutions, booking systems, project management tools, internal business tools, and custom enterprise applications tailored to your specific business needs.

Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive deployment services on platforms like AWS, Vercel, and DigitalOcean, plus ongoing maintenance packages that include updates, security patches, bug fixes, feature enhancements, and 24/7 monitoring to keep your application running smoothly.
