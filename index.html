Case Study

Personal Finance Dashboard & Payment Tracker

Our client was struggling to keep track of multiple credit cards with different payment dates, interest rates, and balances. We developed a custom web application with a secure login system that allows them to input all their credit cards in one place. The dashboard displays critical information like upcoming payments, interest charges, remaining balances, and payment history. The system also calculates how each payment affects interest charges and principal balance to help users make smarter financial decisions.

Payment Organization

Eliminated missed payments with automated reminders and consolidated payment tracking

Financial Visibility

Created complete transparency into interest charges, payment impact, and debt reduction progress

Debt Reduction

Helped the client reduce overall debt by 22% in the first six months through strategic payment planning
