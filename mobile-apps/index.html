Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline varies based on complexity and features. A simple app with basic functionality typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a more complex app with custom features, integrations, and advanced functionality can take 4-6 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you develop for both iOS and Android?

Yes! We develop native apps for both iOS and Android platforms. We build with React Native, which allows us to build a single codebase that works on both platforms, potentially reducing development time and cost.

How much does mobile app development cost?

App development costs vary significantly based on features, complexity, design requirements, and platform choice. Simple apps can start around $5,000 while more complex apps with custom features, backend infrastructure, and advanced functionality can be $10,000 or more. We provide detailed quotes after our initial consultation.

Will I own the source code and intellectual property?

Absolutely! Once the project is completed and payment is received, you own 100% of the source code and all intellectual property rights. We'll transfer everything to you, including documentation, and can assist with publishing to the App Store and Google Play Store.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and updates?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your app running smoothly. This includes bug fixes, OS updates, security patches, feature enhancements, and technical support. We'll work with you to create a maintenance plan that fits your needs and budget.
