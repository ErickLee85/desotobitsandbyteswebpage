Chart.js

Bar Charts

Bar charts provide a straightforward way to compare values across different categories, ideal for visualizing data like sales figures or survey responses.

Line Charts

Line charts are perfect for tracking changes over time, such as monitoring trends in stock prices or website traffic.

Pie & Doughnut Charts

Pie and doughnut charts are excellent for displaying parts of a whole, making them ideal for showing percentage distributions or market shares.

Radar Charts

Radar charts are useful for comparing multiple variables across different categories, often used in performance analysis and skill assessments.

Polar Area Charts

Polar area charts are similar to pie charts but allow for the comparison of multiple variables, suitable for data sets with several categories.

Bubble & Scatter Charts

Bubble and scatter charts visualize relationships between variables, helping to identify correlations and patterns in data sets.
