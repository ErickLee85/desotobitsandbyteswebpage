Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a company website?

A typical company website takes 2-4 weeks from start to finish. This includes design consultation, development, content creation, revisions, and launch. More complex sites with custom features or e-commerce may take 4-8 weeks.

Will my website work on mobile phones and tablets?

Yes! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile, we ensure your site looks perfect and functions flawlessly on all screen sizes.

Do you help with domain names and hosting?

Yes! We can help you register a domain name, set up reliable hosting, configure email addresses, and handle all the technical setup. We work with top-tier hosting providers to ensure your site is fast, secure, and always available.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your site updated, secure, and running smoothly. Whether you need content updates, new features, or technical support, we're here to help your website grow with your business.
